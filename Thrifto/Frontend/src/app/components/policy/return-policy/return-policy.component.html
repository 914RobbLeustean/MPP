Return Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2024

Return Window

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Return Requirements

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In the same condition as you received it
  • Unworn and unwashed
  • With original tags attached
  • In the original packaging

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Intimate apparel and swimwear
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags
  • Gift cards

Return Process

  1. Log into your Thrifto account and navigate to your order history
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return and initiate a return request
  3. Print the provided return shipping label
  4. Package your items securely with all tags and original packaging
  5. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location

Return Shipping

Standard Return

  • Free returns within 14 days
  • $7.99 fee after 14 days
  • Processing time: 5-7 business days

Express Return

  • $14.99 flat rate
  • Expedited processing
  • Processing time: 2-3 business days

Refund Process

Once we receive your return:

  1. We will inspect the item(s) to ensure they meet our return requirements
  2. You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed
  3. The refund will be issued to your original payment method
  4. Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item:

  1. Contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos of the damaged item or proof of incorrect item
  3. We will provide a prepaid return label and process your refund or replacement immediately
