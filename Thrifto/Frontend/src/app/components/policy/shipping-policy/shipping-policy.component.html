Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2024

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Shipping Options

Standard Shipping

  • Delivery within 5-7 business days
  • $4.99 for orders under $50
  • Free for orders over $50

Express Shipping

  • Delivery within 2-3 business days
  • $9.99 flat rate
  • Available for all orders

Next Day Delivery

  • Next business day delivery
  • $14.99 flat rate
  • Order must be placed before 2 PM EST

Shipping Locations

We currently ship to all 50 United States and select international destinations. For international shipping rates and delivery times, please contact our customer service team.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order status at any time by:

  • Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Logging into your Thrifto account and viewing your order history
  • Contacting our customer service team with your order number

Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver all orders within the specified timeframes, occasional delays may occur due to:

  • Severe weather conditions
  • Natural disasters
  • Shipping carrier delays
  • High volume periods (holidays)

We will notify you of any significant delays affecting your order.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or damaged during transit:

  1. Contact our customer service team within 48 hours of the expected delivery date
  2. Provide your order number and any relevant photos of damaged items
  3. We will investigate the issue and work to resolve it promptly
